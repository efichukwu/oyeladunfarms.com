Our Background

Who we are

Oyeladun Farms is a leading agricultural farm in Nigeria and registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (RC 1213015). It was established in 2014 in Ibadan, Oyo state and its processing factory at Incubation Centre, Oba Ogunji Road, Agege, Lagos State. OYELADUN FARMS LTD IS ONE OF THE COMPANIES THAT MAKE UP BAYO OYEDEJI GROUP BAYO OYEDEJI GROUP HAS A STRONG HOLD ON BUSINESSES RANGING FROM REAL ESTATE TO FOOD PROCESSING, TO AGRIBUSINESS, ETC. BAYO OYEDEJI AND CO (Estate surveyors and valuers) Chattered firm real estate consultants registered with the Nigeria Institutions of Estate Surveyors and Valuers BOYCOP SERVICES Private security guard consultants and provider registered with the Nigeria Security and civil defense corps GLAMOUR AND ESTATE Property development company LADUN WATER - The Production of sachet and bottled water (NAFDAC certified)
